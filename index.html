Welcome to the Chatbot Page

Our product is a chatbot app with an intuitive user interface that calculates the carbon footprint from human activities and suggests practical ways to reduce it, providing tailored solutions to help individuals and businesses minimize their environmental impact.

Click the bottom-left message icon to start chatting with the bot. Click it again to minimize the chat window.

⚠️ Please note: This chatbot operates on a limited free plan, which means it can run out of credits. To ensure uninterrupted service, we kindly ask that you minimize the number of questions and consider combining multiple questions into longer prompts when possible. Thank you for your understanding!
